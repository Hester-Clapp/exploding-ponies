This game is based on the card game Exploding Kittens. Players take it in turns drawing cards until somepony draws a villain card. When that happens, they are defeated and are out of the game. The aim is to be the last player once everyone else has been defeated.

Every player starts with 8 cards, one of which is guaranteed to be an Element of Harmony. On your turn, you can play as many cards as you want, or none at all. You end your turn by drawing a card from the draw pile.

In there deck, there are enough villain cards to eliminate all but one of the players. If you draw a villain card, you must play it immediately. If you don't stop it with an Element of Harmony card, you will be defeated.

Elements of Harmony can be used to defeat villains. If you drew a villain card, play one of these on top, then you can banish the villain back to the draw pile. You get to choose where in the draw pile you put it.

If another player does something you don't like, play a nope card on top to stop them. You can play these at any time, even if it's not your turn. You can play a nope on top of another nope to cancel out its effect and create a "yup". You cannot play a nope on a villain card or an Element of Harmony.

Playing an attack card will end your turn and also force the next player to take 2 turns. If you are the victim of an attack, you can play another attack card to end both of your turns and make the next player take 4 turns.

Playing a skip card will let you end your turn without drawing a card. If you are the victim of an attack, a skip will only end one of your turns.

Play a see the future card to look at the top 3 cards of the draw pile.

If you see a villain card on top, you can play a shuffle card to shuffle the draw pile.

Favor cards let you ask another player to give you a card. After playing a favor card, click on the player you want to ask - their name will turn blue.

If someone asks you for a favor, click on the card you want to give them. You can also play a nope card to stop the action.

The remaining cards are pony cards, of which there are 5 different types. If you play two of the same pony as a pair, you can steal a random card from another player. After playing two pony cards, click on the player you want to steal from.
